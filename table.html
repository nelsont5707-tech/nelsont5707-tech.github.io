Timeline of William Blake’s Works

Year Work Description
1789 Songs of Innocence A collection of lyrical poems reflecting purity and childhood.
1794 Songs of Innocence and of Experience Juxtaposes innocence with the harsh realities of experience.
1790–93 The Marriage of Heaven and Hell Philosophical work combining poetry, satire, and visionary art.
1804–20 Jerusalem Blake’s longest prophetic book, filled with rich symbolism.
